Project-TYPOLOGY

crowdsource hyperlapse

Using over 500 images from instagram public posts, this hyperlapse have been created. With combined work hour of 24hrs and apropro use of Adobe Fresco for image placement & for stop motion.

Davinci Resolve was used to add beautiful color grading, gradient title motion, and audio-video mixing. This creative is best experienced with audio as it uses city audio clips synchronized with background music to elevate the immersive experience.

Typologies is serires started be @samthecobra over instagram who used over 1500 publicly posted images to create hyperlapse to show across New York.

This sort of hyperlapse creative requires immense patience. Also the most time consuming part is the collection of images from the internet. Here for the typologies of metro, delhi and mumbai, I used instagram #search and geolocation search to amass each frame of the video.
